About Me

Curiosity about life in all its aspects, I think, is still the secret of great creative people.

Leo Burnett
My name is Liza Gaylord. I started coding at the age of eleven and secured an internship working in software during high school. I turned a passion for computer science and a set of diverse interests into an acceptance to MIT. I recently graduated with a double major in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (a joint major) and Mechanical Engineering. I am currently seeking a job in product management because I want to make my natural role a full-time career.
I have never been content with doing my piece and going home for the day; I am interested in the larger picture. With every group project I have faced, I took on the responsibility of ensuring the product was delivered on time and to specification (often one I wrote). I usually described this role as "team lader," though rarely assigned and never with actual authority. In truth, this role was product management; it is what I have always done, and what I would like to continue doing.
